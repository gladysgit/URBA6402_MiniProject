The Problems

Not just a means of communication, social media nowadays is a combination of information ranging from textual, graphical, temporal, spatial to social reactions.
In Hong Kong, Instagram ranks third among all social media apps. The hashtag culture in Instagram make it an excellent source to provide insight on the changes over time and space, which can facilitate the context analysis for urban planning.
Why people would like hiking? Where would they go? Let's find the answers from the data.
